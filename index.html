I recently completed my undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering from Pulchowk Campus, IOE, Tribhuvan University. My focus is on projects that can directly benefit a large number of people and contribute to accelerating positive changes in society.

Currently:My aim is to enable 3D creation with iterative controls for creative individuals to better match their imaginations and streamline their iterative workflow.

My 2030 goal: I envision enabling people to watch the 2030 World Cup live as holograms through augmented reality, facilitated by real-time scene reconstruction using RGB videos. (Why I choose this particular problem ?)

My long-term goal: To create AI models (accurate representation of our complex, 3D and dynamic world) that can teach any concept to any person through any modality, thereby making education universally accessible and transformative.
